About the Conference

The International Conference on Materials for Electronic, Energy, Quantum, and Biomedical Technologies (MQUBIT) is a multidisciplinary event that convenes researchers, scientists, academicians, and industry professionals worldwide to discuss recent advancements in Materials Science and Technology, including cutting-edge Quantum Computing technology. MQUBIT possesses considerable technical significance owing to the intersection of various advanced research fields, including flexible electronics and ultrafast computing. The aim of this conference is to foster productive scientific collaboration, discourse, and discoveries across various domains in Applied Materials Science. Conference participants will have the chance to examine pioneering research through keynote addresses, plenary sessions, invited talks, and both oral and poster presentations, thereby connecting theoritical discoveries with pratical applications
